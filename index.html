Apple News

Launched with iOS 9 on September 16, 2015

News collects all the stories you want to read, from top news sources, based on topics youâ€™re most interested in. It's designed to be a rich and immersive experience, letting you enjoy stunningly crafted articles that reflect the style of the publications they come from. (Apple)
It tailors the experience to all Apple devices and allows for exploration and discovery of content. At it's heart, it's a news reader with an Apple user experience and a footprint on every device.
The more you read, the better News gets at understanding your interests, refining the selection of stories delivered to your screen so they are relevant to you. Easily share articles with others and save them to read offline. It has a footprint on all of your user's Apple devices. (Apple)
